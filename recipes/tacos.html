Tacos

Description

Mexican Cooking. a tortilla filled with various ingredients, as beans, rice, chopped meat, cheese, and tomatoes, and folded over in half or rolled into a loose cylinder shape

This chicken tacos recipe is the perfect set-it-and-forget-it dinner.

ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Gather all of the ingredients
  2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  4. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
